Common Issues & Solutions

Try these steps: (1) Clear your browser cache and cookies. (2) Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your credentials. (3) Ensure you're using the correct email address. (4) Disable browser extensions that may block login. If the issue persists, contact support with your account email.

Data sync issues are often caused by: (1) Slow or unstable internet connection. (2) Integration token expiry — re-authenticate your connected apps. (3) Browser cache — try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R). (4) Check our status page for any ongoing sync issues on our end.

If you believe you've been charged incorrectly, please: (1) Review your billing history in Settings → Billing. (2) Check if a plan upgrade or add-on was applied. (3) Contact our billing team at billing@support.com with your invoice number. We process refund requests within 3–5 business days.

Performance issues can be resolved by: (1) Refreshing the page or restarting the app. (2) Clearing browser cache and cookies. (3) Trying a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). (4) Checking your internet speed. (5) Disabling heavy browser extensions. If the problem continues, report it via the feedback button.

If you haven't received the verification email: (1) Check your spam or junk folder. (2) Ensure you entered the correct email address. (3) Click "Resend Verification Email" from the login screen. (4) Add noreply@ourapp.com to your contacts to prevent filtering. If still not received after 10 minutes, contact support.
