Forgot your password?

EaseMail uses your firm's Microsoft 365 account for authentication. Password resets are managed directly through Microsoft's secure account recovery portal.

Why Microsoft Account Recovery?

EaseMail does not store or manage passwords. Your credentials are secured and managed by your organization's Microsoft 365 tenant, ensuring enterprise-grade security and compliance.

Go to Microsoft Account Recovery

Quick tip

You'll be redirected to account.microsoft.com. Enter your firm email address and follow the on-screen instructions to verify your identity and reset your password.

What you may need

Your firm email address
Phone number linked to your account
Microsoft Authenticator app (if enabled)
